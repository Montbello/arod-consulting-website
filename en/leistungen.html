My Approach

How My Consulting Works

No off-the-shelf solutions. Every collaboration starts with the person — and only ends when real results are visible.

01

1:1 Conversation & Needs Analysis

We begin with a deep one-on-one conversation. I ask targeted questions, listen carefully and analyse: What are your current needs? Where are the real deficits — not the surface-level symptoms? What is costing you energy, time or money every day?

The result is an honest assessment of your situation — without sugarcoating, without hasty solutions.

02

Tailored Tools & Personal Development

Based on the analysis, I develop two things in parallel:

Tailored Software Tools

Where your company or you personally have gaps, I develop precisely fitting digital tools. No standard software that fits 80% — but tools that close exactly your gaps. CRM systems, dashboards, automations, internal apps.

Personal Development Programme

Business success starts with the person. We jointly identify which habits are counterproductive and which are beneficial — and develop a concrete learning plan: In which areas should you deepen your knowledge? What blocks you, what drives you?

03

Apply for Business Funding

Only once you have gone through the first two phases — once the foundation is right, the tools are installed and the personal development is underway — do we jointly apply for public funding.

BAFA (German Federal Business Consulting Grant) and INQA fund exactly this type of consulting. Through the structured preparation in phases 1 and 2, your application is well-founded, complete and has significantly higher chances of success. You receive up to 80% of consulting costs back — retroactively for the entire collaboration.
