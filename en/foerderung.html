BAFA

BAFA — German Federal Business Consulting Grant

The Federal Office of Economics and Export Control (BAFA) funds business consulting for small and medium-sized enterprises. You get up to 80% of costs reimbursed.

Who Is Eligible?

  • Companies from the founding stage (including the first 2 years)
  • Companies with up to 249 employees
  • Annual turnover under €50 million or balance sheet total under €43 million
  • Companies in economic difficulties (special conditions)

What Is Funded?

  • Business development and business model development
  • Strategy consulting and growth planning
  • Process optimisation and digitalisation
  • Marketing and sales strategies
  • Financial planning and controlling

Conditions

  • Funding rate: up to 80% of consulting costs
  • Maximum grant: €3,200 (net)
  • Maximum daily rate: €4,000 net
  • Application must be submitted before consulting begins

What I Handle

  • Checking your funding eligibility (free of charge)
  • Preparing and submitting the BAFA application
  • Delivering the funded consulting
  • Usage verification and final invoice
INQA

INQA Coaching — Organisational Development

The Initiative for New Quality of Work (INQA) funds consulting on staff management, work organisation and operational transformation for SMEs.

Who Is Eligible?

  • Companies with up to 249 employees
  • Non-profit organisations and associations
  • Companies undergoing organisational change

What Is Funded?

  • Organisational development and team structures
  • Leadership development
  • Operational transformation processes
  • Digital work organisation
  • Employee retention and company culture

Conditions

  • Funding rate: up to 80%
  • Maximum grant: €7,500
  • Up to 5 consulting days possible
  • Can be combined with BAFA funding

Ideal for

  • SMEs building their team structure
  • Growing companies with new employees
  • Owners looking to delegate responsibility
  • Businesses in restructuring phases
