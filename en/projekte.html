FinTech · Web-App · Germany

SchuldenKompass

Over 6 million people in Germany are over-indebted. Most don't know where to start. SchuldenKompass gives them a clear digital navigation — step by step out of the debt spiral.

USPNo comparable German digital product for private individuals
USPSimple, anonymous, without a consultation appointment
USPAutomated authority and form navigator

The Problem

Over-indebtedness is a growing problem — for private individuals as well as solo self-employed and small businesses. Existing help offerings are often confusing, bureaucratic, with long waiting times and hard to access — especially for people in situations of stress and shame.

The result: many people act too late. Debts grow, the situation worsens, personal insolvency becomes the only option.

The Solution

SchuldenKompass offers structured, fully digital navigation: record debts, prioritise them, understand solutions and implement them step by step — no appointment, no waiting time, no stigma.

Type Web-App / Mobile
Market 6+ million affected in Germany
Status In Development

Core Features

  • Debt Overview — structurally record and categorise all liabilities
  • Priority Analysis — automatically: which debts to address first (ranked by interest, garnishment risk, deadline)
  • Solution Path Cards — clear explanations of instalment payments, negotiation, consumer insolvency
  • Progress Tracking — visible progress motivates continuation
  • Authority Navigator — which offices, which forms, which deadlines
  • Anonymous Mode — no registration needed for first steps
Food · Gastronomy · Franchise

Sabor a mi Tierra

Sabor a mi Tierra — "Taste of My Homeland" — brings authentic Latin American cuisine to Hamburg. From the first food truck, a scalable franchise concept is emerging for further cities.

USPAuthenticity — no fusion cuisine, real recipes
USPFranchise-ready model with standardised processes
USPCultural message: food as a bridge between cultures

About the Concept

Latin American cuisine in Germany is often diluted, adapted and thereby stripped of its soul. Sabor a mi Tierra stands for the opposite: original recipes from Venezuela, Colombia, Peru and other Latin American countries — with the ingredients, techniques and spirit of the source cuisine.

The concept combines street food culture with family warmth. Every dish tells a story — of origin, journey and arriving in Hamburg.

Model Food Truck → Catering → Franchise
Cuisine Venezuelan, Colombian, Peruvian
Status Active

The Franchise Model

  • Replicable — fully standardised processes, recipes and quality standards
  • Scalable — concept designed for Hamburg and further German cities
  • Community Anchor — each location embeds itself in the local Latin American community
  • Event Catering — festivals, corporate events, weddings
  • Hammerpeace Partner — official gastronomy partner of the festival

Sport · e.V. · Hamburg

Alma Latina e.V.

The only Hamburg sports club that specifically supports Latin American athletes — with American Football as the main sport and the Olympics 2028 as a concrete goal.

USPOnly LatAm sports club in Hamburg with Olympic ambitions
USPConnects sport with cultural identity and community
USPAmerican Football: growing sport with Olympic debut in 2028

About the Club

Alma Latina e.V. gives Latin American athletes in Hamburg a sporting home. Many of these talents have no access to the right networks, training infrastructure and funding structures within the German sports system.

The club changes that: it connects sport with cultural identity and gives athletes the infrastructure, network and support they need for a professional career.

American Football was included as an Olympic discipline in Los Angeles 2028 — making the coming years the decisive qualification phase for many players.

Main Sport American Football
Location Hamburg
Goal Olympics Los Angeles 2028
