Professional Consulting for Your Business — The Government Pays 80%

You have a business idea or a small company? You want to set it up professionally but don’t know exactly how? There are government funding programs that help you — and I guide you through the entire process.

Why do you need this?

Because you don’t have to figure it all out alone

Maybe you have a great business idea — or you’re already running a small company. But you’re wondering: How do I set this up properly? How do I get customers? How does accounting work? What software do I need?

That’s exactly why there are two German government funding programs — BAFA and INQA. The government pays 80% of the consulting costs because it wants small businesses to succeed. You get an experienced consultant by your side who explains everything, builds systems for you, and teaches you how to run your business. You don’t need to be an expert — that’s my job.

What exactly do I do for you?

Program 1: BAFA — Building Your Business

The BAFA program (Federal Office for Economic Affairs) helps you set up your business properly from the ground up. Here’s exactly what we do together:

  • We analyze your idea or business togetherWhat’s working? What’s missing? Where are the opportunities? I assess everything with you.
  • I create a business plan for youSo you know exactly where your business is heading — and you can present it to banks or investors.
  • We build a marketing strategyWebsite, social media, Google — I show you how to find customers and make your offer visible.
  • I set up your accounting and financial planningRevenue, expenses, taxes — you’ll have your numbers under control with no nasty surprises.
  • We digitize your processesInvoices, customer management, scheduling — with the right tools you save time every day.
  • I show you which software and tools you needNo expensive wrong purchases — only what truly fits your business.
What exactly do I do for you?

Program 2: INQA — Professionalizing Your Business

INQA Coaching (Federal Ministry of Labor) is for businesses that are already running and want to take the next step. 12 consulting days over 4–7 months — intensive and hands-on:

  • We optimize your workflowsWhere is time wasted? Which steps are unnecessary? We make your daily operations more efficient.
  • I train you and your team in new methodsModern work methods that immediately work in practice — no dry textbook knowledge.
  • We set up a performance dashboard (KPIs)So you can see at a glance how your business is doing — revenue, customers, costs, everything transparent.
  • I guide the introduction of new systemsWhether inventory management, CRM, or accounting software — I set it up and show you how it works.
  • We build your leadership skillsHow do you motivate employees? How do you delegate effectively? I coach you as a leader.
  • You receive a complete action plan at the endA concrete roadmap for the next 12 months — so you know exactly what comes next.

Who can participate?

The Requirements Are Simple

BAFA — For Startups & Young Businesses

  • You have founded a company (even brand new)
  • Fewer than 250 employees
  • Annual revenue under €50 million
  • Even if you’re currently in financial difficulty
  • All industries welcome

INQA — For Established Businesses

  • Your company has existed for at least 2 years
  • 1 to 249 employees
  • Registered in Germany
  • You want to improve or digitize processes
  • All industries welcome
